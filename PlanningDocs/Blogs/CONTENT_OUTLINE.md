```
HEADER: The Factor Report | Week of [Date]

SECTION 1: Factor Pulse (2-3 paragraphs)
- Quick summary of factor performance this week
- Which factors led/lagged
- Notable factor regime changes

SECTION 2: Market Through a Factor Lens (3-4 paragraphs)
- One key market event analyzed via factors
- What it means for different portfolio types
- Actionable insight

SECTION 3: Chart of the Week
- One compelling visualization
- Brief explanation

SECTION 4: Ask the Analyst
```
